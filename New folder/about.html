About MTG Boats

Our Story

Founded in 2025, MTG Boats began as a small family-owned boat dealership with a passion for the water. What started as a modest operation with just three boats in inventory has grown into one of the region's most trusted marine dealerships, serving thousands of satisfied customers.

Our Mission

At MTG, we're committed to helping you find the perfect vessel for your aquatic adventures. Whether you're a first-time buyer or a seasoned captain, we provide honest advice, quality boats, and exceptional after-sales service.

Why Choose Us?

  • 20+ years combined marine industry experience
  • Rigorous 100-point inspection on all used boats
  • Flexible financing options available
  • Full-service marina with maintenance and repairs
  • Local knowledge of best boating spots
